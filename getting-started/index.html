How to Join Amtgard in 5 Easy Steps

1

Find a Chapter Near You

The first step is to locate your nearest Amtgard Chapter (also called a "Park"). We have Chapters across Canada that meet regularly, usually on weekends.

Scroll down to see our Chapter locations, or use the Chapter finder page to see each Chapter's meeting times and locations.

There is also a way to use your location to find the closest Chapter:

play.amtgard.com

2

Attend Your First Park Day

Just show up! No reservation needed. We recommend wearing comfortable clothes and closed-toe shoes. For your first visit, you don't need to bring anything special - we have loaner equipment for newcomers.

When you arrive, introduce yourself as a new player, and someone will help get you started.

3

Sign a Waiver

Amtgard is a physical activity, so all players need to sign a waiver. If you're under 18, you'll need a parent or guardian to sign for you.

You can download the waiver below and bring it with you, or sign one when you arrive.

Download Waiver
4

Learn the Basics

During your first few visits, you'll learn:

  • Basic combat rules and safety
  • How to use foam weapons
  • Different character classes and their abilities
  • Game mechanics and scenarios

Don't worry about learning everything at once - our experienced players will guide you through the process.

Download the Rulebook
5

Create Your Own Equipment

Once you've decided to join regularly, you'll want to make or acquire your own equipment:

  • Foam weapons (we can teach you how to make these)
  • Medieval/fantasy clothing (called "garb")
  • Optional: shields, spell components, etc.

Check out our equipment guides for detailed instructions on making your own gear.

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to play Amtgard?

Amtgard is free to play! There are no mandatory fees to participate in weekly park days. Some players choose to pay optional dues, $10 for each six (6) month period, to support the chapter and gain voting rights, but this is not required to play.

Is Amtgard safe?

Safety is our top priority. All weapons are made of foam with a lightweight core and must pass safety inspections. We have strict rules about hit strength and target areas. Like any physical activity, there is some risk of minor injuries (bruises, twisted ankles, etc.), but serious injuries are rare.

What age do I need to be to play?

Players must be at least 14 years old to participate in combat. Children under 14 can participate in non-combat activities with parent/guardian supervision. Players under 18 need their waiver signed by a parent or guardian.

Do I need to be athletic or in good shape?

Not at all! Amtgard welcomes players of all fitness levels and abilities. While combat can be physically demanding, there are many roles and classes that don't require high athleticism. You can participate at your own pace and comfort level.

What should I wear for my first day?

For your first visit, comfortable clothes you can move in and closed-toe shoes are all you need. Athletic wear, t-shirts and sweatpants, or similar casual clothes are perfect. Medieval garb is not required for newcomers, though you'll likely want to start acquiring or making some as you continue playing.
