About Legends Library

"Legends Library is the Barrie Amtgard Group"

Amtgard is a live action Fantasy Boffer Sport and Battlegame Community. Engage the enemy in battle games to win glory for your team/household. Don't like to battle? There are various tournaments to compete in, from Arts to clothing, to blacksmithing. Bards sing tales of the great and mighty around fires. There's a lot to experience and little to lose.

Legends Library has been part of Amtgard since 2024.
