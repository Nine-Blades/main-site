About Felfrost

"Hogsback Moose Pack!"

Felfrost is the Ottawa chapter of the Kingdom of the Nine Blades, serving the National Capital Region.

We welcome players of all experience levels, from complete beginners to seasoned veterans. Our community is known for its friendly atmosphere, creative artisans, and competitive fighters.

Whether you're interested in combat, roleplay, arts and sciences, or just making new friends in a medieval fantasy setting, Felfrost has something for everyone.
