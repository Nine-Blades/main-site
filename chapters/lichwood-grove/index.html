About Lichwood Grove

"Built close to a forest that had bad rumors surrounding it."

In addition to thrilling militia and class-based combat, Amtgard offers leadership and service opportunities, mentorship, engaging role-playing, awards and accolades, crafting of all kinds, and so much more.

It's free to play, easy to learn, and we have spare equipment for beginners. Just drop in to find out more.

Lichwood Grove has been part of Amtgard since February 2016.
