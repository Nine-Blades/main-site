About Bellhollow

"Brantford Amtgard LARP!"

We play according to the rules of Amtgard, a world-wide organization with medieval and fantasy combat sports using padded foam weapons. With both fantasy and authentic clothing, to immerse players in a world of heroic combat, quests, crafts, and more. It's free to play, easy to learn and we have lots of spare equipment. Come on out and give it a try!

Bellhollow has been part of Amtgard since Feb, 2016.
