About Twilight Peak

"Twilight Peak has served Amtgard for Two Decades!"

Though never the strongest field in the Kingdom, Twilight Peak has proven its resilience and determination, with its players embodying that same unwavering spirit! Known for its dedicated and passionate members, Twilight Peak creates a vibrant and welcoming community for all.

We welcome players of all experience levels, from complete beginners to seasoned veterans. Our community is known for its friendly atmosphere, creative artisans, and competitive fighters.

Whether you're interested in combat, roleplay, arts and sciences, or just making new friends in a medieval fantasy setting, Twilight Peak has something for everyone.
