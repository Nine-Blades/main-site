About Linnagond

"Medieval, Magical Fantasy Live Action Role Play"

We are always looking for more players to come participate in games and activities so please come out and join us some time, we would love to have you! No prior experience or equipment needed, Amtgard is Free to Play (14+ to play).

In addition to the meeting times, check out our Google calendar of Linnagond Events

Linnagond has been part of Amtgard since August, 2012.
